National Health Stack

ABDM Integration

Dignidad HMIS is being developed to support integration with the Ayushman Bharat Digital Mission (ABDM), enabling secure and consent-driven digital health data exchange.

  • ABHA ID Creation
  • Health Record Linking
  • Consent-Based Data Sharing
  • Support for OPD and IPD workflows

The platform is being prepared in alignment with ABDM guidelines and is intended to function as a HIP (Health Information Provider) and HIU (Health Information User) upon integration.

Our mission

Built for Indian healthcare

Dignidad HMIS is a scalable Hospital Management Information System (HMIS) built for the Indian healthcare ecosystem — from single-clinic OPD practices to multi-specialty hospitals running full OPD and IPD operations.

The platform delivers hospital-grade clinical and administrative workflows with ABDM-ready architecture — supporting national digital health standards, facility accreditation, and readiness for participation in the Ayushman Bharat health stack.

  • Unified OPD appointments and IPD admission management
  • Cloud-ready, modular hospital-grade architecture
  • ABDM-ready today — on track to become a fully integrated Ayushman Bharat digital health platform.
